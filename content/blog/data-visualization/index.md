This publication presents a technical reference for the identification and selection of native and adaptive plant species suitable for urban environments.

Developed as part of OPAMSS initiatives, the catalog supports urban reforestation and landscape design efforts in the San Salvador Metropolitan Area, providing criteria for species selection based on ecological function, adaptability, and urban conditions.

## Overview

Urban vegetation plays a key role in improving environmental quality and enhancing ecosystem services in cities. This catalog provides guidance for selecting species that are suitable for urban conditions while contributing to biodiversity and resilience.

It serves as a practical tool for planners, municipalities, and designers involved in urban forestry and green infrastructure projects.
